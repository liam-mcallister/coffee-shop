Earn More With Coffee Shop Rewards

Join Coffee Shop Rewards to earn free drinks, exclusive offers and more. Enjoy all of these great Coffee Shop rewards when you register your loyalty card.

Every time you use your Coffee Shop loyalty card or our mobile app when you’re buying something at a participating store, you’ll earn a Star. These Stars add up to earn you exclusive Coffee Shop rewards, like free drinks and more. The more Stars you collect, the more rewards you earn.

It’s easy to join, too. All you need to do is:

  1. Pick up a loyalty card in store
  2. Shop and earn rewards

Once you have an account you can keep track of how many Stars you’ve collected on our website or mobile app. When you collect enough Stars to earn a reward, we’ll send you an email.

It can take up to 24 hours to complete the loyalty card registration process. But don’t worry, we’ll get your rewards to you as soon as we can.
